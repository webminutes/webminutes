FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can you really deliver?

Most landing pages and static sites are delivered within 1–2 days. E-commerce sites take 2–3 days. Complexity and responsiveness of client feedback can affect timelines slightly.

Do I need to provide content?

Ideally yes — your text, logo, and images help us personalize quickly. If you don't have these ready, we can use AI-generated placeholder content and swap it later.

What happens after delivery?

You receive full access to all files and hosting. You can manage it yourself, or sign up for one of our Maintenance plans for ongoing support.

Can I see a demo before paying?

Yes. We show you a working demo before any payment. You only pay when you're happy with the direction and want us to finalize and deploy.

What technology do you use?

For static sites: HTML/CSS/JS hosted on GitHub Pages or Vercel (free). For larger projects: Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and Supabase. We choose what's best for your needs.

Are prices negotiable for students?

We already price for students — our starting rate of 500K VND reflects that. For group/club projects or competition use, contact us and we'll work something out.
